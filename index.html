About Chanderi

Chanderi, in Madhya Pradesh, is famous for its handwoven sarees, history, and culture. From royal forts to the fine artistry of fabric, Chanderi is a true gem of Indian tradition.

Types of Chanderi Sarees

Cotton Chanderi

Perfect for summers, these sarees are lightweight and breathable with elegant patterns.

Silk Chanderi

Rich in texture and sheen, silk chanderis are ideal for weddings and traditional functions.

Zari Work Saree

Decorated with golden zari, these sarees reflect royal charm and timeless elegance.
